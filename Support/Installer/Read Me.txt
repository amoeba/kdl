KDL Installation Read Me
========================

**WARNING**
This is a pre-release preview release of the KDL. It is likely that there will
be bugs, and it is advisable that you do not work with anything critical and
have back ups.

========================

To complete installation of KDL simply double click the "Install" script, and
KDL will be installed on to you system. If you have an existing version of KDL
installed, then it will be upgraded.
